Verteil Technologies

Our vision is to build a customer centric new-generation distribution platform which will enable travel suppliers to innovate and sell new products and services, allowing consumers to access these with the least effort and cost. Our direct connect platform will provide customers a fully transparent travel experience by allowing them to take informed decisions comparing product offers from different suppliers against a wide range of attributes.

We believe that the travel distribution world is on the verge of a major transformation. We at Verteil plan to play a big part in this.




Value Proposition

AGENCIES

  • Provide rich service and accurate product information to customers.
  • Maximize revenues by selling complete selection of ancillary products
  • Improve customer service.
  • Increase productivity by reducing shopping 'think time' and process automation

SUPPLIERS

  • Promote disintermediation and reach out to consumers directly
  • Take control of your customer journey with relevant, context specific offers
  • Sell complete portfolio of products
  • Intelligent caching mechanisms ensures minimal load on supplier infrastructure.

CUSTOMERS

  • One stop shop for travel related products
  • Ability to choose a travel experience instead of just buying products
  • Ability to shop and compare the value of these propositions across airlines
  • Understanding true value of airline products

CERTIFICATION


Verteil Technologies is a "NDC Capable" Level 3 certified IT provider as per the IATA NDC certification program. Level 3 certification is the highest level NDC certification instituted by IATA, the body that represents and serves the airline industry.

IATA Travel Agency Partnership


Iata Agents Association of India (IAAI) one of the largest IATA agency consurtiums in India signs an exclusive partnership with Verteil Technologies to be their NDC direct connect technology partner. Verteil Technologies NDC direct connect platform “Verteil Direct Connect” will be integrated with IAAI’s ITop booking portal to deliver new innovations to the travelling public.

Verteil Technologies NDC direct platform, Verteil Direct Connect (VDC) has been successfully integrated with British Airways NDC platform, which makes Verteil Technologies now an authorized IT provider for British Airways worldwide.
